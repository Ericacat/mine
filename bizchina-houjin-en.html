BizCHINA
Corporate Training Management

I added a corporate training management feature to the existing B2C Chinese language learning platform.
From recruitment to completion and certificates, I designed the full flow from scratch — so HR managers can move through each step naturally, all in one place.

Challenge

The Problems

Staff Carrying It All Alone

Sending reminders, tracking completion, issuing certificates — all done manually by staff. Each company had different situations, and staff had to handle each one individually, with no consistent way to stay on top of it all.

Psychological Burden on HR Personnel

Reaching out to employees with low completion rates is a sensitive task when HR staff work in the same hierarchical workplace. It puts pressure on individuals. There was a clear need to hand this responsibility off to the system.

No Visibility into Training Progress

To check each company's training status, staff had to ask internal team members directly. There was no way to see real-time progress.

Solution 1

Designing the Training Flow as One Coherent Journey

For the first time, the full training flow — "Create recruitment page → Employee applies → Takes the course → Certificate issued" — exists as a single, connected experience. Every step that staff once handled separately can now be completed in one place.

Recruitment Page Editor

Corporate HR staff can set their own plans, discounts, and publication dates, then create and publish an application page for employees. A real-time preview on the right side gives instant feedback on every change.

Solution 2

A Dashboard Where Status Is Felt, Not Just Seen

The most important thing this dashboard communicates is completion rate. Low rates draw the eye through color — naturally, without scanning. The layout is designed so managers can sense the state of things without needing to scroll.

Email Notification Toggle

The system sends reminders automatically — HR doesn't have to be the one to nudge. By removing the need for direct personal outreach, I created a space where attendance can be encouraged without anyone feeling pressured.

Solution 3

Bulk Certificate Download

When certificates are needed, managers can select multiple employees and download them together — no issuing one by one. For deadline-driven tasks like subsidy applications, this matters. Past training records are also available to review at any time.

Results & Impact

Outcomes and Learnings

Quantitative Results

  • What staff once handled individually by hand is now handled by the system
  • Certificates can be retrieved and distributed whenever they're needed

Qualitative Results

  • HR no longer has to initiate awkward conversations — the system handles the follow-up
  • Each company's training status is visible at a glance, at any time

Reflection

I started every decision from "who is opening this screen, and how are they feeling?" The reminder feature in particular was shaped by the reality of workplace relationships — a design choice that acknowledges the emotional context, not just the functional one.
