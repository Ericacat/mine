BizCHINA
Corporate Training Management

I added a corporate training management feature to the existing B2C Chinese language learning platform.
The entire workflow — from recruitment to attendance tracking and certificate issuance — was previously handled manually by staff. I designed and built a system to centralize and automate it.

Challenge

The Problems

Staff Doing Everything Manually

Tasks like sending attendance reminders, calculating completion rates, and issuing certificates — things the system should handle — were all done by hand. Every company had different needs, so the workload kept growing for the staff managing them.

Psychological Burden on HR Personnel

Reaching out to employees with low completion rates is a sensitive task when HR staff work in the same hierarchical workplace. It puts pressure on individuals. There was a clear need to hand this responsibility off to the system.

No Visibility into Training Progress

To check each company's training status, staff had to ask internal team members directly. There was no way to see real-time progress.

Solution 1

Designing and Systemizing the Training Workflow

I designed the full training flow as a system for the first time: "Create recruitment page → Employee applies → Completes the course → Certificate is issued." Everything that staff used to handle case by case can now be completed within the dashboard.

Recruitment Page Editor

Corporate HR staff can set their own plans, discounts, and publication dates, then create and publish an application page for employees. A real-time preview on the right side gives instant feedback on every change.

Solution 2

At-a-Glance Status and Early Detection of Low Completion

The most important metric in this dashboard is completion rate. To help managers spot problems fast, low completion rates are highlighted with color warnings. The layout is designed so managers can understand the full picture without scrolling.

Email Notification Toggle

The system can automatically send reminder emails to encourage attendance — no HR involvement needed. By removing the human cost and the awkwardness of "following up," I created an environment where HR can promote attendance without hesitation.

Solution 3

Bulk Certificate Download

Instead of issuing certificates one by one, managers can select multiple employees and download them all at once. This is especially important for time-sensitive tasks like subsidy applications. Past recruitment data and completion records are also available in the "Past Training" page for easy review.

Results & Impact

Outcomes and Learnings

Quantitative Results

  • Reduced manual management workload for internal staff
  • Shortened response time for certificate issuance

Qualitative Results

  • HR personnel can now manage reminders entirely within the system, reducing psychological burden
  • Real-time visibility into each company's training status

Reflection

This project went beyond UI design. I started from who would use this screen and in what context. The reminder notification feature in particular was a design decision shaped by the reality of workplace dynamics — an effort to solve not just a UI problem, but the operational and psychological challenges behind it.
