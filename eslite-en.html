Eslite Online
EC Site Redesign

I redesigned the online store of Taiwan's famous bookstore brand, Eslite.
The store changed from selling books only to a full lifestyle shop. I worked on the design direction, mobile layout, and the buying steps.

Role
Web Designer
Period

Project Type
EC Site Redesign

Eslite rebranded from an online bookstore to a full shopping site at the end of 2020. I redesigned the whole site to show more products and make shopping easier.

Challenge

Challenges

From a Bookstore to a Lifestyle Shop

Eslite is well known as a bookstore. But their physical stores also sell lifestyle goods and gifts. The challenge was to show these products well online, just like books.

Specific Problems

  • The old layout was made for books. It did not show lifestyle products well.
  • The design did not work well on mobile phones.
  • The buying steps were too long. Many users gave up before finishing.
  • The in-store and online member accounts were not connected.
Solution

Design Approach

1. Wide Layout for the Homepage

I used a wide 1280px layout and changed the banner positions. Users could quickly find featured products. Lifestyle goods and gifts looked just as good as books.

Design Points

  • Wide layout creates a strong visual impression
  • Banner positions are set by product category
  • Easy paths for users with different goals

2. Responsive Web Design

I worked with the frontend team to use responsive web design. One code works for all devices. This also helped with SEO.

Results

  • Works well on desktop, tablet, and mobile
  • Faster to develop (one codebase)
  • Better search ranking with SEO

3. One Product Page for Both Books and Goods

I made one product page layout that works for both books and goods. I added more product information to help users decide.

Design Points

  • One flexible layout for books and lifestyle goods
  • More product details for users
  • Better related product suggestions

4. Shorter Buying Steps

I put users first and made the buying steps shorter. Now there are only 3 steps to finish a purchase.

Improvements

  • Reduced to 3 simple steps
  • Less information needed at each step
  • Users can see how far they are at a glance

5. App Design: In-Store and Online Together

The app redesign made online shopping better. It also kept the in-store member features. Users could use the same account in stores and online.

Results

  • One membership for in-store and online
  • Use points and coupons in both places
  • A connected online and offline experience
Results & Impact

Results & Learnings

Project Results

  • The site became a full lifestyle shop, not just a bookstore
  • Mobile users had a much better experience
  • The shorter buying steps improved conversion
  • In-store and online members now share one account

What I Learned

  • I led a large EC site redesign from start to finish
  • I worked closely with the frontend team to build responsive design
  • I learned how to improve conversion with user-focused design
  • I understood how design connects online and offline experiences

Reflection

Going from a bookstore to a full lifestyle shop was a big challenge. The best result was building a design that kept Eslite's brand identity while serving many more user needs.
