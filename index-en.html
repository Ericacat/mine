About

As a designer, I believe there is always a better way.

When users say "this is hard to use" or "I don't get it," I want to understand why — and find a way for it to feel natural. Experiences are made by people, so there is always a more intuitive form waiting to be found.

This shapes how I work. When a design needs to come to life precisely, I write the frontend code myself. When design alone can't bridge a gap, I sit down with the team and stakeholders to find a direction everyone can move forward with.

There is always a better way. I keep working to find it.
